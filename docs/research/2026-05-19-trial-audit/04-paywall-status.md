## Important corrections to V's task list

1. **CSPS.com** — V's list said "NEJM 2019." It was published in **Lancet Neurology 2019;18(6):539–548**. NEJM never published this trial.
2. **THEIA** — V's list said "JAMA Neurol 2024." Full publication appears in **Lancet Neurology 2025**, not JAMA Neurology 2024. Preliminary results were *presented* at World Stroke Congress Oct 2024 but not published in JAMA Neurology. If V has a different THEIA reference in mind (a different JAMA Neurol paper), please clarify before authoring.
3. **CREST-2** — Now published (NEJM Nov 21, 2025). 6-month NEJM embargo lifts ~May 21, 2026 — essentially today. Recheck PMC within the next 1–2 weeks for free full-text release.
4. **ESCAPE-NEXT** — Confirmed Lancet 2025 (Hill et al.). Paywalled.
5. **ANNEXA-I PMID** — could not confirm a definitive PMID in this pass; V's institutional access portal will resolve it via DOI 10.1056/NEJMoa2313040.
