Headache pattern finder — v4.2 (Frame 1 + Frame 3 re-skin)

Concept: live differential narrowing — the clinician describes one feature at a time and watches the shortlist of likely headache types narrow. The narrowing is the probability display. No percentages.

Step 1 of ~6 · Safety check

Before pattern-matching: any of these red flags?

Read the list. These point to a secondary cause that needs workup before any pattern fits.

SNNOOP10 warning signs

  • Thunderclap onset
    Peaks in seconds, "worst headache of life"
  • First headache after 50
    New or changed pattern in an older patient
  • Focal deficit or seizure
    Weakness, confusion, vision loss, or a fit
  • Fever or systemic illness
    Neck stiffness, immunosuppression, or cancer
  • Pregnancy or postpartum
    Any stage, or within 6 weeks of delivery
  • Papilloedema
    Swollen optic discs on fundoscopy
  • Triggered by cough or exertion
    Valsalva, straining, or positional change
  • Painkillers ≥10–15 days/month
    Possible medication-overuse headache

For reading, not selecting. SNNOOP10 warning signs (Mitsikostas 2017).
